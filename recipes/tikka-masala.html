Paneer Tikka Masala

Home

Description

Paneer Tikka masala is a popular alternative dish to chicken tikka masala for vegetarians and pescetarians. Filled with fragrant spices and a creamy sauce, this dish is perfect for everyone as the spice level can be adjusted and the paneer can be substituted for chicken to create the original recipe. If put in the time and effort, you will end up with the perfect dish for families or to save and eat later.

Ingredients:

Marinade:

  • Paneer
  • Hung curd
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Kashmiri Red Chili Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Kasuri Methi
  • TTurmeric
  • Lemon Juice
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Cashews
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Salt and Sugar
  • Heavy Cream
  • Coriander Leaves

Instructions:

  1. Add the yogurt, red chili powder, ginger garlic paste, garam masla, turmeric, kasuri methoi, oil, lemon juice, and salt to a mixing bowl. add more salt if needed
  2. Add the paneer to the marinade and ensure that the cubes are coated.
  3. Dice the onions and bell peppers to the size of the paneer and add to the marinage.
  4. Saute the onions with the oil and the tomatoes, cashews, sugar, and salt until soft.
  5. Add in red chili powder & garam masala and saute for 2 mins.
  6. Cool the mixture and blend it with water.
  7. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger garlic paste. Saute for 1-2 mins.
  8. Pour the puree in the pan. taste test and adjust
  9. Add water until the consistency is like gravy and cook for 10 mins to thicken. stir occasionally
  10. Once thick, remove the whole spices and add kasuri methi by crushing and sprinkling. add salt if needed
  11. Bake, grill, or fry the paneer and vegetables until crisp.
  12. Add these to the gravy with the cream. optionally add garnish
  13. Serve with rice, roti, or naan.
